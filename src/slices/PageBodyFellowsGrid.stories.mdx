##### Page

# Fellows Grid

Grid of Fellows, each with their portrait, name, and cohort number and a link
to a biography. Fellows are displayed in two tabs: one displaying just the most
recent cohort, and another displaying fellows from all cohorts.

#### Slice fields

This slice has no fields.

### Typical usage

By default, just the current cohort will be displayed. The user can click the
"Forum of Fellows" button to toggle to the full list of Fellows.
